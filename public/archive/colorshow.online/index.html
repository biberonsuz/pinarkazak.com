This project started in January when the 2nd year students revisited the Color Circle by creating a narrative based on a meaningful concept. Unfortunately, due to the drastically changed circumstances, the presentations in the Academy were not possible anymore. As an answer to this, the students were asked how an exhibition of their projects could look like without any restrictions at all, such as budget, location, facilities, assistance and time. Each of them progressed their project further, finally visualized in the Color Show. Pınar Kazak and Paulina Trzeciak designed this wonderful website which allows us to travel around the globe, visiting all the exhibitions and reading about the projects. I want to thank Pinar and Paulina for their great contributions and all students for their positive and inspiring working spirit.

Enjoy the tour!
