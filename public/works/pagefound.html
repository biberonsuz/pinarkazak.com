PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

Page Found was an exhibition of redesigned books by second year KABK Graphic Design students, planned to take place in Page Not Found, the Hague. Given the circumstances of the COVID-19 Pandemic in March 2020, this was not possible, and an online exhibition took place, with the launch of the web platform.

The were also several posters designed for the opening event, which were first designed to be printed, but later were used on social media to inform of the event.
