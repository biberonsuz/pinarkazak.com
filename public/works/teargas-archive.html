PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

The #teargas Archive is an online research project looking into the use of social media, for news reporting in the information age. The research process looked into the concept of 'artificial clouds', both in terms of cloud storage, and in terms of cloud forms; smoke, explosion, teargas. At the intersection was digital reports of events representing tragedies and violence.

The work explores our relation to these two understandings of artificial clouds, focusing on 'teargas' and 'online archive', specifically Instagram and Twitter archives. The web app includes periodic scrapings from the Twitter and Instagram Search API's, with the keyword '#teargas'. The client side of the app is the result of these scrapings, which is a proposition for a postmodern way of news reporting, dependant on individual right to digital communication and online archiving, on the topic #teargas.
