Shah Sans is a python generated variable font, made with Python in Robofont. Inspired by the shape shifting folklore of the middle east, Shahmaran, it has a wide range of variance in width, thickness and roundness. This ongoing project explores the structure of variable fonts in terms of engineering, design and generation, which in the future, will help generate other fonts using different constructions. The typeface was developed with the supervision of Just van Rossum.
