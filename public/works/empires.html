PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

EMPIRES is a double-part work in the form of a 30 minute livestream and a Unity WebGL game, in collaboration with Emir Karyo Yoaf.

An analysis of Empires, on a dyanmic live experience utilising the technologies of today, telling the stories of Empires of the past. What Empires have looked like in relation to the soil that is called Turkey today.

EMPIRES Livestream Event

Following the Livestream performance, the Empires Online Game explores the Empires of the future, inspired by the Empires of the past, and of today. Dreaming of a world in 2071, the concept of citizenship, communication and basic human needs are explored.
