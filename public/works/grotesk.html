GROTESK is a fashion collection developed in collaboration with Weronika Uyar.
Computer generated patterns, constructed from geometric letters, typographically inspired. Incorporating colour as a dynamic aspect in pattern making, the GROTESK collection encapsulates hundreds of patterns, ready to be made into a collection in the future.
