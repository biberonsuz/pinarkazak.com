PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

A Conversation With Astrid Florentinus, is a printed interview publication, in conversation with Astrid who is the workshop assistant at the RISO Workshop in Royal Academy of Art, the Hague (KABK). I was interested in her work and personal experience with the workshop, after being familiar with the workshop for a year and working with her. The design is found raw content from the workshop made into a RISO printed publication. This appropriative style makes each printed publication unique, in composition and colour.

A CONVERSATION WITH ASTRID FLORENTINUS PRINT MAKING, RISO, PUBLICATION 2019.
