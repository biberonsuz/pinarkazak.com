PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

Alexipharmakon is a digital typeface, designed utilising calligraphic techniques. First designed as a calligraphic script, it was later turned into a lower-case typeface with Glyphs. Developed with the supervision of Guido de Boer.
