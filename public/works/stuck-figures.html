PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

Stuck Figures are a style of drawing inspired from the feeling of feeling stuck. First developed as drawings of stick figures stuck between text, they later developed into RISO prints, ink stamps, and later an installation. Specifically the installation plays with the idea of color and opacity, and speaks of the uneasy feeling of feeling stuck in an abundance of space. This idea was later explored again using the painting medium, playing more with the idea of space, as opposed to the idea of opacity. It was also later developed into a tote bag, going back to the idea of transparency, and later developed into transparent and colourful coasters.
