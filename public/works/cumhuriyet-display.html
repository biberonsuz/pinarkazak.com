PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

Cumhuriyet Display is a revival typeface, studied and inspired by the title lettering of Cumhuriyet Newspaper, from 1930's to 1940's. Taken from the archive of the Cumhuriyet Newspaper, it is inspired from the 10 letters available in the title. Still in development. Developed with the supervision of Matthias Kreutzer.

Made with Glyphs.
