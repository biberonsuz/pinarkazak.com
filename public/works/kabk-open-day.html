PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

The visual identity was comissioned by the Graphic Design Department of the KABK, for their Open Day in 2021. The visual identity was a collaboration with Weronika Uyar, and Paolo Vigliotti. As the information and details for the identity were not available at the time of designing, the identity was designed and translated into a browser tool to create content for instagram posts and stories by the client at a later time.
