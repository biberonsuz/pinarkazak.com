PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

The Color Show is a website that is made up of the works of 2nd Year Graphic Design students at the Royal Academy of Art, the Hague (KABK) in spring 2020. The works are the result of individual projects in the theme of "color", for the Image class in the curriculum guided by Michel Hoogervorst.
