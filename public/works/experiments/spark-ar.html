PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

I wanted to experiment with Instagram face filters through the app Spark AR Studio. I was mainly interested in the tool as I was thinking that Instagram filters could be used as design tools for Instagram brands or influencers, if explored fully and the users were provided an appopriate UI. Following this, I created a colour filter that is expandable in terms of customisation.
