PINAR KAZAK WORKS ABOUT

Desert On The Sun is a print series of 60 prints and a book of the same name. They are early works exploring colour and composition as well as mixed-media printmaking techniques such as RISO and silkscreen. Using the versatility of each technique, they speak of surreal landscapes. Landscapes of lonely solitude, landscapes of pleasant confusion. Each copy is unique (an edition of one).
Some copies were sold on the 2019 Royal Academy of the Art, The Hague (KABK) Open Day.
